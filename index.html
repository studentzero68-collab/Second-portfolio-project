About me

Building things that actually matter

I am Mukelani, a 20-year-old Full Stack Web Developer in training studying at iHub Africa, based in Gauteng, South Africa.

I didn't come from a background that handed me everything so I learned early that if I want something I have to go and get it myself. Web development is that thing for me right now. I started coding because I needed something that was mine — something I could build a future on.

What drives me is building things that actually matter — charity platforms, community pages and experiences that make people feel something. I am quick to adapt, I don't mind making mistakes and I don't pretend to know everything. I just keep going.
