About me

Designing sites people actually want to use

I am Mukelani, a 20-year-old website designer in training at iHub Africa, based in Gauteng. I did not start with a clear path into tech, so I learned to build my own — one layout, one fix, and one deployed project at a time. I care about clean structure, readable typography, and layouts that feel intentional on every screen size. My work so far includes Tesla, Netflix, and YouTube clones, plus apps like a To-Do List and Baker's Delight. I am open to internships, freelance work, and collaborations where I can keep growing as a designer who ships real projects.
